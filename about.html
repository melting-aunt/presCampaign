A bit about me


First off, let me make one thing clear. I am a great man. The greatest. Greatly. So that's settled then, and no-one thinks I'm overcompensating. Over the years, I have climbed Everest, been to Mars (It's actually blue! But NASA doesn't want you to know that!) and fought lions, but one thing has eluded me - the undying respect of millions. So I have decided to run for president of the United States and I have also decided to win, because quite frankly, I reckon it'd be a good laugh.


"I love having a laugh!"
- Louis de Pol, haver of laughs


In my spare time, I am having a lot of fun learning to code. I'm doing this because after my unconstitutional 20 year spell as POTUS, I am planning on becoming a tech billionaire like Zuckerberg, or the Napster guy. I also believe this will aid me as POTUS, as when the CIA boffins are showing me those black-and-green screens about hacking I will understand about what they mean.
