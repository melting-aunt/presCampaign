The Olive Party

The Olive Party was founded by Louis de Pol, Founder of the Olive Party, in 2023. The etymology of the Party comes from two sources. Louis de Pol, Liker of the Colour Olive Green, is a big fan of the colour and associated pallette, as he thought it would look good on his website. He had also eaten a pizza with olives on it after his friend gave him a Jazz Cigarette and he decided olives were really great, and decided to just go with it.
